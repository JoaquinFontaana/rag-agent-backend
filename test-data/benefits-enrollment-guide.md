## 1. Health Insurance

### 1.1 Medical Plans

We offer three medical plan options through Blue Cross Blue Shield:

#### Gold PPO Plan
- **Monthly Premium:** 
  - Employee Only: $150
  - Employee + Spouse: $380
  - Employee + Children: $350
  - Family: $520
- **Deductible:** $500 individual / $1,000 family
- **Out-of-Pocket Max:** $3,000 individual / $6,000 family
- **Primary Care Visit:** $25 copay
- **Specialist Visit:** $40 copay
- **Emergency Room:** $150 copay
- **Prescription Drugs:** $10/$30/$60 (Generic/Preferred/Non-preferred)

#### Silver PPO Plan
- **Monthly Premium:**
  - Employee Only: $100
  - Employee + Spouse: $280
  - Employee + Children: $250
  - Family: $420
- **Deductible:** $1,500 individual / $3,000 family
- **Out-of-Pocket Max:** $5,000 individual / $10,000 family
- **Primary Care Visit:** $35 copay
- **Specialist Visit:** $60 copay
- **Emergency Room:** $250 copay

#### Bronze HDHP (High Deductible Health Plan)
- **Monthly Premium:**
  - Employee Only: $50
  - Employee + Spouse: $180
  - Employee + Children: $150
  - Family: $320
- **Deductible:** $3,000 individual / $6,000 family
- **Out-of-Pocket Max:** $6,000 individual / $12,000 family
- **HSA Eligible:** Yes (see section 1.3)

### 1.2 Dental Insurance

Delta Dental PPO:
- **Monthly Premium:**
  - Employee Only: $25
  - Employee + Spouse: $60
  - Employee + Children: $55
  - Family: $85
- **Annual Maximum:** $2,000 per person
- **Preventive Care:** 100% covered (no deductible)
- **Basic Services:** 80% covered after $50 deductible
- **Major Services:** 50% covered after $50 deductible
- **Orthodontia:** 50% covered (lifetime max $1,500)

### 1.3 Vision Insurance

VSP Vision Care:
- **Monthly Premium:**
  - Employee Only: $10
  - Employee + Dependents: $20
- **Eye Exam:** $10 copay annually
- **Lenses:** $25 copay annually
- **Frames:** $150 allowance every 24 months
- **Contacts:** $150 allowance (in lieu of glasses)

### 1.4 Health Savings Account (HSA)

Available only with Bronze HDHP:
- **Company Contribution:** $1,000 (employee only) / $2,000 (family)
- **Annual Contribution Limits (2024):**
  - Individual: $4,150
  - Family: $8,300
  - Age 55+ catch-up: Additional $1,000
- **Investment Options:** Available for balances over $2,000
- **Triple Tax Advantage:** Tax-free contributions, growth, and withdrawals for medical expenses

## 2. Life and Disability Insurance

### 2.1 Basic Life Insurance
- **Coverage:** 1x annual salary (company-paid)
- **Maximum:** $500,000
- **Accidental Death & Dismemberment:** Equal to life insurance amount
- **No medical exam required**

### 2.2 Supplemental Life Insurance
**Employee:**
- Coverage options: $50,000 to $500,000 (in $50,000 increments)
- Guaranteed issue: Up to $150,000 (no medical exam)
- Cost: $0.20 per $1,000 of coverage per month (under age 40)

**Spouse:**
- Coverage options: $25,000 to $250,000
- Guaranteed issue: Up to $50,000
- Cost: $0.25 per $1,000 of coverage per month

**Child(ren):**
- Coverage: $10,000 per child
- Cost: $5/month for all children

### 2.3 Short-Term Disability (STD)
- **Company-Paid Benefit**
- **Coverage:** 60% of salary
- **Maximum Weekly Benefit:** $2,000
- **Waiting Period:** 14 days
- **Benefit Duration:** Up to 90 days

### 2.4 Long-Term Disability (LTD)
- **Company-Paid Benefit**
- **Coverage:** 60% of salary
- **Maximum Monthly Benefit:** $10,000
- **Waiting Period:** 90 days
- **Benefit Duration:** To age 65

**Optional Buy-Up:**
- Increase to 70% of salary
- Cost: 0.5% of salary per month

## 3. Retirement Plans

### 3.1 401(k) Plan

**Eligibility:** Immediate upon hire

**Employee Contributions:**
- Pre-tax and Roth 401(k) options
- 2024 Limit: $23,000
- Age 50+ catch-up: Additional $7,500
- Can contribute 1% to 90% of salary

**Company Match:**
- 100% match on first 3% of salary
- 50% match on next 2% of salary
- Maximum company match: 4% of salary
- Vesting: Immediate

**Investment Options:**
- Target-date funds (default option)
- Index funds (domestic and international)
- Bond funds
- Company stock (up to 20% of portfolio)

### 3.2 Roth 401(k)
- After-tax contributions
- Tax-free withdrawals in retirement
- Same contribution limits as traditional 401(k)
- Company match goes into traditional 401(k)

## 5. Enrollment Process

### 5.1 New Hire Enrollment
- Deadline: 30 days from hire date
- Effective Date: First day of employment
- Complete enrollment online at benefits.company.com
- Username: Your employee ID (see offer letter)
- Temporary Password: Last 4 of SSN + DOB (MMDD)

### 5.2 Annual Open Enrollment
- Period: November 1-30 annually
- Changes effective: January 1
- Can change any benefit elections
- If no action taken, coverage continues as-is (except FSA)

### 5.3 Qualifying Life Events
You may change benefits mid-year for qualifying events:
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of child
- Death of dependent
- Change in employment status (yours or spouse's)
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in dependent eligibility

**Action Required:** Within 30 days of event

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I change my benefits during the year?**  
A: Only during open enrollment or within 30 days of a qualifying life event.

**Q: When do my benefits start?**  
A: First day of employment for new hires. January 1 for open enrollment changes.

**Q: What if I miss the enrollment deadline?**  
A: You'll have to wait until the next open enrollment unless you have a qualifying event.

**Q: Can I waive health insurance?**  
A: Yes, but you must show proof of other coverage.

**Q: How do I file a claim?**  
A: Most claims are filed electronically by your provider. For reimbursements, file through the insurance company's website or app.

**Q: What happens to my benefits if I terminate?**  
A: Coverage ends on your last day of employment. You'll receive COBRA information within 14 days.
