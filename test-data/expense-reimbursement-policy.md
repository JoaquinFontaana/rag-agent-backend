# Expense Reimbursement and Travel Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** Finance Department  
**Version:** 2.8

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for business-related expenses and travel to ensure appropriate use of company funds while providing employees with necessary resources to perform their jobs effectively.

## 2. General Principles

### 2.1 Business Necessity
- Expenses must be necessary and reasonable for business purposes
- Choose cost-effective options without sacrificing safety or productivity
- Consider virtual alternatives to travel when appropriate
- Obtain pre-approval for significant expenses

### 2.2 Documentation
All expenses require:
- Original itemized receipts
- Business purpose description
- Names of attendees (for meals and entertainment)
- Submission within 30 days of incurring expense
- Manager approval before reimbursement

## 3. Travel Arrangements

### 3.1 Booking Process
- Book through company travel portal (TravelCorp)
- Book at least 14 days in advance when possible
- Compare options and choose reasonably priced alternatives
- Corporate credit card preferred over personal reimbursement

### 3.2 Approval Requirements

| Expense Type | Pre-Approval Required |
|--------------|----------------------|
| Domestic flights | No (if within policy) |
| International flights | Yes |
| Conference registration over $1,000 | Yes |
| Rental car | Manager approval |
| First/Business class | VP approval required |

## 4. Transportation

### 4.1 Air Travel
- **Domestic flights:** Economy class
- **International flights under 6 hours:** Economy class
- **International flights over 6 hours:** Premium Economy allowed
- **Flights over 10 hours:** Business class with VP approval
- Book 14+ days in advance for best rates
- Baggage fees reimbursed (one checked bag)

### 4.2 Ground Transportation

#### Rental Cars
- Intermediate or compact class only
- Book through company portal for insurance coverage
- Decline rental agency insurance (company card covers)
- Return with full tank to avoid inflated charges

#### Personal Vehicle Mileage
- Current IRS rate: $0.67 per mile
- From office to destination (not home to office)
- Submit mileage log with dates, destinations, business purpose

## 5. Lodging

### 5.1 Rate Guidelines

| City Type | Maximum Nightly Rate |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Tier 1 (NYC, SF, London, Tokyo) | $300 |
| Tier 2 (Major cities) | $200 |
| Tier 3 (Other locations) | $150 |

### 5.2 Incidental Charges
**Reimbursable:** Wi-Fi, business center fees, parking at hotel  
**Not Reimbursable:** In-room movies, mini-bar, spa services, room upgrades

## 6. Meals and Entertainment

### 6.1 Per Diem Rates - Daily Allowance

| Meal Period | Allowance |
|-------------|-----------|
| Breakfast | $15 |
| Lunch | $20 |
| Dinner | $35 |
| Total Daily | $70 |

### 6.2 Business Meals
Meals with clients, prospects, or candidates:
- List all attendees in expense report
- Document business purpose
- Get itemized receipt

**Reimbursement Limits:**

| Meal Type | Per Person Limit |
|-----------|------------------|
| Breakfast | $25 |
| Lunch | $40 |
| Dinner | $75 |

## 7. Corporate Credit Cards

### 7.1 Usage Guidelines
**Approved Uses:** Business travel, client meals, business supplies, conference registrations  
**Prohibited Uses:** Personal expenses, cash advances, lending to colleagues

### 7.2 Reconciliation
- Submit expense report within 15 days of statement close
- Attach receipts for all charges over $25
- Unexplained charges may lead to card revocation

## 8. Expense Submission Process

### 8.1 Expense Report Requirements
Use Expensify platform to submit:
- Date of expense, category, business purpose, amount
- Attached receipt
- Manager approval

### 8.2 Receipt Requirements
- All expenses over $25
- All hotel charges, airfare, rental cars
- Photos of receipts (clear and legible)

### 8.3 Timeline
- Submit within 30 days of expense date
- Late submissions require director approval
- Expenses over 90 days old will not be reimbursed

### 8.4 Reimbursement
- Processed within 10 business days of approval
- Direct deposit to employee's bank account

## 9. Policy Violations

Violations may result in written warning, expense denial, corporate card revocation, or disciplinary action up to termination.
